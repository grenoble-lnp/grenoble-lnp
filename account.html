Cyber Security Tips: What Not to Do

  • Do not use the same password for multiple accounts.
  • Do not click on suspicious links or attachments in emails.
  • Do not share your personal information on unsecured websites.
  • Do not ignore software updates and patches.
  • Do not use public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.
  • Do not leave your devices unattended in public places.
  • Do not download software from untrusted sources.
